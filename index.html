🚀 Procedure Steps

1.
Open: Settings → About Phone → Version Info
2.
Tap Build Number 7 Times To Enable Developer Options.
3.
Navigate To: Settings → System → Developer Options
4.
Enable The Following: • OEM Unlocking and • USB Debugging
5.
Connect Your Device To The PC Using A USB Cable.
6.
Open The **Platform Tools** Folder.
7.
Click The Address Bar, Type: 
cmd
Then Press **Enter**.
8.
Verify Device Connection: 
adb devices
A USB Debugging Authorization Prompt Will Appear On Your Device. Select **"Allow"**.
9.
Reboot To Fastboot Mode: 
adb reboot bootloader
10.
Once The Device Enters Fastboot Mode, Execute: 
fastboot flashing unlock
11.
A Confirmation Screen Will Appear On The Device. Use **Volume Up** To Confirm Bootloader Unlocking.
12.
Wait For The Unlock Process To Complete.
13.
Reboot The Device: 
fastboot reboot
14.
The Device Will Automatically Perform A **Factory Reset**.

⚠️ WARNING & SAFETY INSTRUCTIONS

  • Data Loss Alert: Unlocking The Bootloader Will **Permanently Erase** All User Data.
  • Create A **Complete Backup** before proceeding with the process.
  • The **First Boot** after unlocking may take several minutes to complete.
  • **OTA updates** may fail or be blocked on modified recovery configurations.
  • Incorrect flashing procedures can result in a **non-booting (bricked)** device.
  • Proceed only if you fully understand the software risks involved.
